About Us


Who We Are

We are a group of four people from a college called the National College of Ireland. Our main goal is to spread awareness about the climate change going and how we can mitigate that with our urban lifestyles. We think its very important that even if you dont live in a city you can make a change in small ways. This websites goal is to share information that benefits all of us. We are also are showcasing our coding skills too. In this webpage we will discuss the one of the 17 SDG goals set by the UN, Sustainable Cities and Communities. We will cover housing, walkable cities and generally all things that fall under urban sustainablity. There is 4 research pages each with their own themes and written by each of our group. We do hope that you enjoy and read our well cited sources coming from academic sources about urban sustainability.





Our Papers

In the first page we will be covering affordable housing and specifically the use of the SDG goal 11 in Tanzania.

In the second page we will be looking at the positive effects of good urban sustainability and what that looks like when implemented correctly.

The third page will be looking at environmental impact of Ireland with studies of traffic in Dublin City
